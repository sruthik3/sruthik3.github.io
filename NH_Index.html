Effect of Global Warming on Climate Change


The earth's climate has changed throughout history which resulted in various cycles of glacial advance and retreat. The abrupt end of last ice age 11,700 years ago marks the beginning of modern climate era and of human civilization. Climate change in this modern era is of significance because most of it is extremely likely to be the result of human activity since the mid 20th century and proceeding at a rate that is unprecendented over decades. In this analysis, we are going to visualize how global surface temperature rise is one of the compelling evidence towards climate change.
